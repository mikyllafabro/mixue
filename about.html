About Mixue Ice Cream Store

Welcome to Mixue Ice Cream Store, your go-to place for delightful ice cream and refreshing coffee. Established with a passion for quality and flavor, our shop offers a wide variety of ice cream flavors and premium coffee blends.

Our Story

At Mixue, we believe in the joy of indulging in the finest ice cream and coffee. Our dedicated team handcrafts each batch with love and care, ensuring you experience the perfect balance of taste and texture. Whether you're a fan of classic flavors or adventurous blends, we have something special for you.

Our Location

Visit us at our store in Taguig and enjoy a cozy ambiance while savoring our delectable treats. We're open daily from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, so drop by anytime to satisfy your cravings.
